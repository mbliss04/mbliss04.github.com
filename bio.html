Born in the Midwest, raised in the West, moved to the East, McCall Bliss is a twenty year old with an eclectic past looking forward to a creative future.

Unlike most kids who aspired to be president when they were little, McCall had the goal of becoming a professional gift wrapper. Sadly, that dream died when her curiosity got the best of her and she tried a bunch of new things. Everything from filmmaking to songwriting.

Currently McCall Bliss is a student at Tufts University in Medford, Massachusetts. She is studying Computer Science with a minor in Communications and Media Studies. The combination of the two fields creates a very interdisciplinary academic experience that she hopes to combine in order to pursue a career filled with creation and innovation.
